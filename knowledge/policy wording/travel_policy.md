# Synthetic Travel Policy Wording — Product Code TRV-STD

**This is a synthetic policy for portfolio purposes. Not a real insurance product.**

## §1 — Period of cover

From the start date shown on the certificate until the end of the insured trip, subject to a maximum of 31 days per trip on annual policies.

## §2 — Medical expenses abroad

Emergency medical and hospital expenses incurred outside the policyholder's country of residence, up to the limit on the schedule. Includes emergency repatriation when medically necessary and approved by our 24/7 assistance line.

### §2.1 Pre-existing conditions
Not covered unless declared and accepted in writing before the trip.

## §3 — Cancellation and curtailment

We pay irrecoverable costs of cancelling or cutting short a trip for reasons including:
- Death, serious injury, or serious illness of the policyholder, a travelling companion, or an immediate family member.
- Jury service or being called as a witness.
- Significant damage to the policyholder's home making it uninhabitable.

Cancellation for "disinclination to travel" (change of mind, fear of travel, work pressure) is not covered.

## §4 — Baggage and personal belongings

Loss, theft, or damage to baggage during the trip. Single-item limit of €500 unless declared. Valuables (jewellery, electronics, cameras) must be carried as hand luggage, not checked.

## §5 — Travel delay

If the scheduled departure is delayed by more than 6 hours due to strike, adverse weather, or mechanical breakdown of the transport, we pay the benefit shown on the schedule.

## §6 — Personal liability

Legal liability to third parties arising during the trip, up to the limit on the schedule. Excludes liability arising from ownership of vehicles, aircraft, or watercraft.

## §7 — General exclusions

- Incidents arising from being under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
- Participation in professional sports or specified hazardous activities unless the Sports extension is listed.
- Travel against official advice from the national foreign affairs ministry.
- Claims for unattended baggage left in a public place.
