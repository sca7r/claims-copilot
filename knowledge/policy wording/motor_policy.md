# Synthetic Motor Policy Wording — Product Code MOT-STD

**This is a synthetic policy for portfolio purposes. Not a real insurance product.**

## §1 — Who is insured

This policy covers the named policyholder and any additional drivers listed on the schedule, when driving the insured vehicle with the policyholder's permission.

## §2 — What is insured

The vehicle described on the policy schedule, including its manufacturer-fitted accessories. Aftermarket modifications are covered only if declared and listed on the schedule.

## §3 — Accidental damage

We cover accidental damage to the insured vehicle, subject to the excess shown on the schedule. This includes collisions with other vehicles, objects, animals, and single-vehicle incidents.

## §4 — Weather and natural events

### §4.1 Flood
Damage caused by flood is covered, provided the vehicle was not knowingly driven into standing water more than 10cm deep.

### §4.2 Storm damage
Damage caused by storm, including falling trees, wind-borne debris, and hail, is covered. A "storm" is defined as wind speeds exceeding Beaufort Force 9 (75 km/h) confirmed by national meteorological data, or equivalent hail/precipitation event.

### §4.3 Exclusion — gradual deterioration
We do not cover damage from gradual weather exposure (rust, paint fade, seal perishing).

## §5 — Theft

Theft of the vehicle, and theft of or damage to parts of the vehicle, is covered. Claims for theft require a police report reference. Items left in an unlocked vehicle are not covered.

## §6 — Third-party liability

We cover the policyholder's legal liability to third parties for death, injury, and property damage caused by use of the insured vehicle, up to the limits shown on the schedule.

## §7 — General exclusions

We will not pay for:
- Damage while the vehicle is being driven by someone not insured under the policy.
- Damage while the driver is under the influence of alcohol or drugs above legal limits.
- Damage caused deliberately by the policyholder or any insured driver.
- Wear and tear, mechanical breakdown, and manufacturer defects.
- Loss of use, unless the optional Courtesy Car extension is listed on the schedule.

## §8 — Excess

The policyholder pays the excess shown on the schedule for each claim, except for windscreen-only claims, which carry a reduced excess of €75.

## §9 — Duty of disclosure

The policyholder must tell us about any change in circumstances that could affect the risk (change of address, additional drivers, modifications, accidents, convictions) within 14 days.

## §10 — Claims process

1. Notify us as soon as reasonably possible.
2. Do not admit liability at the scene.
3. Exchange details with any third parties.
4. Obtain a police report for theft, vandalism, or injury.
5. Provide photographs where safe to do so.
